About AGDAL School

AGDAL School is a prestigious CBSE-affiliated government institution dedicated to providing quality education to students from classes 6th to 12th. Our school stands as a beacon of academic excellence and holistic development, nurturing young minds to become responsible citizens and future leaders.

Established with the vision of making quality education accessible to all, AGDAL School combines traditional values with modern teaching methodologies. We are committed to fostering an environment that encourages critical thinking, creativity, and character building.

Admissions Information

Join our community of learners and embark on a journey of academic excellence

1

Application Form

Download and fill the admission form from our office or website. Submit with required documents.

2

Document Verification

Submit previous academic records, birth certificate, address proof, and passport-size photographs.

3

Entrance Test

Admission Confirmation
